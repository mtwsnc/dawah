A Glimpse into the Religion of Islam

About the Book

Almost everyone is familiar with the universal greeting of Islam, “As- Salaamu Alaikum (Peace be upon you)”. The yearly pilgrimage to Mecca is witnessed by Muslims and Non-Muslims worldwide. Ramadan is recognized as the month wherein Muslims abstain from food and drink from daybreak until sunset. Nearly everyone has a Muslim co-worker, associate, classmate, or relative; however, many people are not well-acquainted with the actual beliefs of Islam.

This is a glimpse into the most misunderstood, misrepresented—yet fastest-growing—religion in the world.

This is Islam.
